01

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ClipFlow ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our automated TikTok video upload service.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care. This policy complies with applicable U.S. privacy laws including CCPA and relevant provisions of GDPR where applicable.

TikTok Data: When you connect your TikTok account, your data is also governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy at tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy.
02

Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address and name (upon registration)
  • TikTok display name and user ID (from OAuth authorization)
  • Billing information (processed via third-party payment processor)

Content & Upload Data

  • Video files temporarily stored during processing and upload
  • Video metadata: captions, hashtags, privacy settings, scheduled time
  • Upload logs and status history

Technical Data

  • IP address, browser type, device identifiers
  • API request logs and error reports
  • Usage analytics (features used, frequency of uploads)
  • Cookies and session tokens (see Section 10)

OAuth Tokens

  • TikTok OAuth access and refresh tokens (encrypted at rest)
  • Token scope and authorization timestamps
03

How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

  • Provide the Service — authenticate with TikTok API, process and upload your videos
  • Account Management — maintain your account, process payments, send receipts
  • Scheduling — queue and execute scheduled uploads at your specified times
  • Error Handling — diagnose upload failures and notify you of issues
  • Security — detect fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Legal Compliance — comply with applicable laws and respond to lawful requests
  • Improvements — analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve the Service

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your video content to train AI models.

04

TikTok API Integration & Data

When you authorize ClipFlow to access your TikTok account, we receive and store:

  • Your TikTok user ID and display name
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens with the scopes you approved

We request only the minimum scopes necessary for the Service to function (content upload permissions). We do not request access to your followers, messages, or analytics.

Token Storage: OAuth tokens are encrypted using AES-256 and stored in isolated, access-controlled databases. Tokens are refreshed automatically and revoked upon account deletion or deauthorization.

You can revoke our access at any time via TikTok → Settings → Security → Apps & Sessions, or by deleting your ClipFlow account.

05

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

  • TikTok — your video content and metadata are transmitted to TikTok's servers via their API for publishing
  • Cloud Infrastructure — hosting providers (e.g., AWS, GCP) for service operation under data processing agreements
  • Payment Processors — for billing (e.g., Stripe); we never store raw card data
  • Analytics Providers — aggregate, anonymized usage data only
  • Legal Authorities — when required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety
06

Data Retention

  • Video Files — deleted from our servers within 24 hours after successful upload or upload failure
  • Upload Logs — retained for 90 days for debugging, then purged
  • Account Data — retained while your account is active; deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • OAuth Tokens — deleted immediately upon account deletion or TikTok deauthorization
  • Billing Records — retained for 7 years as required by financial regulations
07

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256 encryption for OAuth tokens and sensitive data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
  • Role-based access controls limiting employee data access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Incident response procedures with 72-hour breach notification
No system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and applicable authorities as required by law.
08

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction — request we limit processing of your data
  • Opt-out — opt out of non-essential data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at cthoesli@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

09

Children's Privacy

ClipFlow is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at cthoesli@gmail.com.

Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies required for the Service to function (session management, authentication). We use limited analytics cookies to understand aggregate usage patterns. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but some Service features may not function properly without them.
